# AidChain Demo Script

**Duration**: 3 minutes
**Format**: Live demo + narration

---

## Opening (30 seconds)

**[Screen: Title slide with problem stats]**

"Humanitarian aid loses $288 million annually to duplicate payments and fraud. Donors have zero real-time visibility into whether their funds reach actual beneficiaries.

AidChain solves this with milestone-based escrow, AI-powered verification, and privacy-preserving biometric proof—all built on Trustless Work."

---

## Act 1: Deploy Escrow (30 seconds)

**[Screen: Donor Dashboard]**

"I'm a donor funding emergency food distribution in Syria. I'm deploying a 10,000 USDC escrow for 1,000 beneficiaries.

**[Click 'Deploy Escrow']**

The smart contract is deployed on Stellar. Funds are locked. Three milestones: beneficiary registration, aid distribution, and final audit.

**[Point to Escrow Viewer link]**

Anyone can inspect the live escrow state at viewer.trustlesswork.com."

---

## Act 2: AI Verification (45 seconds)

**[Screen: Field Worker panel]**

"Now I'm a field worker on the ground. I've registered beneficiaries and I'm uploading evidence: a photo hash and GPS coordinates.

**[Click 'Upload Evidence']**

Watch the AI agent verify this in real-time.

**[Point to Activity Log]**

The AI checks:
- Photo hash uniqueness—no duplicate evidence
- GPS coordinates—confirms we're in a humanitarian zone
- Timestamp—within the milestone window

**[AI log shows green checkmarks]**

AI approved with 94% confidence. Now it goes to the human field monitor."

---

## Act 3: Biometric Proof (30 seconds)

**[Screen: Biometric Scanner]**

"Here's the breakthrough: privacy-preserving biometric verification.

**[Click 'Scan Beneficiary' multiple times]**

Each beneficiary's iris scan is hashed locally. Only the hash goes on-chain—raw biometric data never leaves the device.

**[Counter increments: 847/1000]**

This proves 847 unique people received aid, without storing sensitive biometric data. Cryptographically impossible to fake."

---

## Act 4: Funds Release (30 seconds)

**[Screen: Field Monitor panel]**

"I'm the field monitor. The AI verified the evidence. I'm doing final human approval.

**[Click 'Approve & Release Funds']**

**[Activity log shows release]**

2,000 USDC released to the NGO. Instant. No banks. No intermediaries.

**[Show milestone progress bar]**

Milestone 1 complete. Two more to go."

---

## Act 5: Hypercert (30 seconds)

**[Fast-forward through remaining milestones]**

"After all milestones complete, a Hypercert is minted.

**[Screen: Hypercert card]**

This is an impact certificate. It shows:
- 1,000 beneficiaries served
- $10 cost per person
- All evidence hashes
- 87/100 impact score

**[Point to 'List for Retroactive Funding' button]**

Other donors can now buy this Hypercert to retroactively reward effective NGOs. This creates a reputation system and a secondary market for proven impact."

---

## Closing (15 seconds)

**[Screen: Architecture diagram]**

"AidChain layers on top of the UN's proven Building Blocks infrastructure—6 million people, 40 million transactions.

We're not replacing humanitarian systems. We're adding programmable accountability.

Built on Trustless Work. Open source. Ready to deploy."

## Demo Tips

### Before Demo
1. Pre-fill form fields to save time
2. Have Escrow Viewer open in another tab
3. Test biometric scanner animation
4. Clear activity log for clean start

### During Demo
1. **Pace**: Speak clearly, don't rush
2. **Visuals**: Point to key UI elements
3. **Activity Log**: Let audience see AI verification in real-time
4. **Escrow Viewer**: Show external verification
5. **Hypercert**: Emphasize the retroactive funding innovation

### After Demo
1. Show GitHub repo
2. Mention open source
3. Invite questions
4. Share Escrow Viewer link
